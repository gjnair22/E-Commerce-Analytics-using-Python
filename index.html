Strategic Actions & Recommendations

Loyalty

Implement Retention Marketing

With a repeat purchase rate of only 3.12%, Olist functions as an acquisition-first marketplace. Creating targeted post-purchase retention email campaigns, segmenting users, and rolling out a membership model can unlock massive Lifetime Value (LTV).

Logistics

Optimise Logistics Node Distribution

São Paulo (SP) represents the heavy majority of customers. However, the late delivery rate is 6.77% and average wait times are 12.1 days. Deploying regional micro-warehouses outside SP (e.g. RJ, MG, and the South) can help slash transit times and delivery volatility.

Pricing

Dynamic Freight and Bundling

Average Order Value (AOV) is R$ 160.58. Integrate smart cross-selling suggestions and recommend item bundles at checkout (e.g., matching low-cost accessories to health/beauty or electronics items) to push AOV values higher.
