# Business Insights

## Executive Summary

This project analyzes the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset to understand customer purchasing behavior, sales performance, payment preferences, product performance, and delivery efficiency. The insights generated can support business decisions related to sales optimization, customer experience, and operational improvements.

---

# Customer Insights

- The business has **96,096 unique customers**.
- Customers are distributed across multiple Brazilian states, indicating a broad customer base.
- Customer location analysis helps identify high-value markets for future business expansion.

---

# Sales Insights

- A total of **99,441 orders** were placed.
- **96,478 orders** were successfully delivered.
- The overall delivery success rate is **97.02%**, indicating a highly reliable fulfillment process.
- Monthly revenue analysis helps identify seasonal sales patterns and business growth opportunities.

---

# Product Insights

- The company sells products across **73 product categories**.
- Product category analysis helps identify high-performing categories that contribute significantly to business revenue.
- Understanding category performance enables better inventory planning and marketing strategies.

---

# Payment Insights

Customer payment preferences:

| Payment Method | Transactions |
|---------------|-------------:|
| Credit Card | 76,795 |
| Boleto | 19,784 |
| Voucher | 5,775 |
| Debit Card | 1,529 |
| Not Defined | 3 |

### Key Observation

- Credit Card is the most preferred payment method.
- Boleto is the second most commonly used payment option.
- Debit Card transactions remain relatively low.

---

# Delivery Insights

- The majority of customer orders were delivered successfully.
- Delivery time analysis helps evaluate logistics efficiency.
- Late delivery monitoring can assist in improving customer satisfaction.

---

# Revenue Insights

Revenue analysis was performed across:

- Product Categories
- Customer States
- Monthly Sales Trends

These analyses help identify:

- High-performing business regions
- Top revenue-generating product categories
- Seasonal revenue fluctuations

---

# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Focus marketing campaigns in high-revenue customer locations.
- Continue optimizing logistics to maintain high delivery success rates.
- Encourage digital payment methods through promotional offers.
- Monitor monthly revenue trends for better sales forecasting.

---

# Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Python can be used for end-to-end business analytics by combining data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, and business insight generation to support data-driven decision making.
